Boy Likes Turtles

The year was 2007 and a ten-year-old boy with a zombie face-paint job was asked by a news reporter in Portland if he liked his new look, his response was: ‘I like turtles’. Those three unexpected words have gone on to attract more than 53 million views on YouTube.

Well, you might be pleased (or worried) to know he’s all grown up, and Jonathan Ware is now back in the world of YouTube as a gamer… who still seems to be obsessed with turtles.In his first video he describes himself as a teen with ‘really bad hair’ and’ an annoying voice’. But we’ll always remember him as the zombie kid who wanted to share with the world his undying love for turtles
